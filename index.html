How to Tune Your Guitar

1. Allow Microphone

Tap "Start Tuning" and grant microphone access when prompted by your browser.

2. Play a String

Pluck a single string on your guitar or bass. The tuner will detect the note automatically.

3. Tune to Green

Adjust the tuning peg until the gauge needle centers and turns green. You're in tune!
